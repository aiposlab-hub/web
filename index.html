The Carte POS Flow

How Data Becomes Profit for Your Restaurant

Order Flow | Front of House

This is the first point of contact with your customer and where all data begins. An intuitive UI and fast order processing enhance customer satisfaction and ensure every piece of customer experience data is accurately captured.

  • Intuitive Menu UI
  • Tableside Ordering & Payment
  • Integrated Dine-in, Takeout & Delivery Management
  • Fast Order Entry & Error Minimization

Ops View | Back of House

Data captured from the Front of House is instantly relayed to the kitchen and back office to automate operations. This ensures stability by preventing missed orders, optimizing inventory, and enabling efficient staff management.

  • Real-time Order Sync (KDS/Kitchen Display System)

  • Staff Permissions & Schedule Management
  • Visual Floor Plan & Table Management

Business Wise | Insight Engine

All operational data converges into the 'Insight Engine.' Here, the restaurant owner transforms from an operator into a strategist who makes decisions based on data. Carte POS is the business brain that grows your restaurant.

  • Real-time Sales Dashboard & Reports
